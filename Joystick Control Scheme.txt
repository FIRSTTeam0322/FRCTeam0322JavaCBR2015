DRIVE_STICK
RobotDrive = X-Axis, Y-Axis, & Z-Axis (Mechanum Drive)

*Brake Control = Button 1 (Trigger)


MANIPULATOR_STICK
Left Stick X-Axis = Left Arm Movement
Right Stick X-Axis = Right Arm Movement

Button 1 = Raise Lift
Button 3 = Lower Lift

Button 2 = Wheels In
Button 4 = Wheels Out

Button 7 [L1] = Wheels Clockwise
Button 8 [R1] = Wheels Counter-Clockwise 

* = Not Implemented
